Me as a Musician

I performed as a solo act in some of the bars here in Baguio. Most of the time it's just me and my guitar all around Baguio City with the songs in my head and a bottle of beer

I've met some great people with great talent. I was lucky enough to meet the YinYang Theory and made me their trainee in vocals and because of that I was exposed to many gigs and many people and they gave me the knowledge and made me a better musician. I've performed with them in Giligan's Island (My First Gig with them), but most of the time I'm performing at 18bc because that is where they have their gigs.

After several months I stopped having gigs with them because I have to return to school

I met a guy named Roland Nimuan and we started to become a duo act and performing around bars here in Baguio City namely, Concoctions, Bohemian, and Dad & I,

I formed a band named "TonyFaith" back in 2011 when I returned to finish my Nursing Course.
